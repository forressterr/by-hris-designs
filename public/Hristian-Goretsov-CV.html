Education

MSc, Human-Computer Interaction
Utrecht University
Utrecht, NL · 2024 – 2026

Thesis — Bridging the User Flows and Data Flows: A Multi-Perspective UX/UI Design Challenge. My thesis investigates how UX and UI design can render hidden system processes — particularly the data flows underlying user-facing software — more visible and intelligible to the people interacting with them. Using Ultra Commerce as a real-world research site, the work examines how user flows and data flows intersect, and surfaces design patterns capable of communicating complex system behaviour clearly. It combines applied practice with academic reflection, contributing to both the industry partner and the broader HCI field.
